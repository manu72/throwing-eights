About Throwing Eights

Born in the vibrant tech scene of Melbourne, Throwing Eights emerged from a shared passion for Magic: The Gathering and data analytics. Our journey began when two enthusiasts, Manu and Jamaica, recognized the need for sophisticated statistical tools in the MTG community.

Our Core Values

Innovation First

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible in MTG analytics, embracing cutting-edge technology to deliver unprecedented insights.

Community Driven

Our Melbourne roots have taught us the importance of community. We're building more than tools - we're fostering a community of data-driven players.

Technical Excellence

With our background in software development and data science, we maintain the highest standards in every feature we deliver.

Our Culture

At Throwing Eights, we blend Melbourne's innovative tech culture with our passion for MTG. Our team culture emphasizes continuous learning, open collaboration, and a deep appreciation for both the technical and creative aspects of game analysis.

Our Vision

Mission

To revolutionize how players understand and interact with Magic: The Gathering through advanced analytics and machine learning, making professional-grade insights accessible to every player.

Where We're Heading

Our goal is to become the global standard for MTG analytics, recognized not just for our technical excellence but for our contribution to the game's competitive evolution and community growth.

Our Impact

We're working to democratize high-level MTG analysis, making professional-grade insights accessible to players of all levels while fostering a more data-informed and competitive community.

Meet Our Founders

Manu

With a background in software engineering and data science, Manu brings technical expertise in machine learning and statistical analysis.

"My goal is to bridge the gap between complex data analysis and practical gameplay insights, making advanced statistics accessible to every player."

Jamaica

Jamaica combines competitive MTG experience with expertise in UX design and full-stack development, ensuring our tools are both powerful and user-friendly.

"I believe that data-driven insights should enhance, not complicate, the gaming experience. We're building tools that I wish I had when I started competing."

Looking Ahead

Growth Strategy

Based in Melbourne's thriving tech scene, we're expanding our team with local talent while building a global presence in the MTG analytics space.

Technology Roadmap

Our development focuses on advanced AI/ML capabilities, real-time analysis tools, and enhanced mobile experiences, always prioritizing performance and accessibility.

Upcoming Features

We're developing new tools for tournament preparation, meta-game analysis, and collaborative deck building, all powered by our next-generation analytics engine.
