About Throwing Eights

Our Story

Throwing Eights emerged from the intersection of competitive gaming and data science. Founded by two passionate MTG players with deep expertise in software development and analytics, we identified a crucial gap in the Magic: The Gathering community: the need for sophisticated, data-driven tools to enhance gameplay and deck building.

Our company combines advanced statistical analysis with practical gaming experience, creating solutions that bridge the gap between raw data and actionable insights. This unique approach has positioned us as pioneers in the MTG analytics space.
