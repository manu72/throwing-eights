About Us

Building Tomorrow's Digital Solutions Today

The Company

Throwing Eights is a dynamic software development company based in the heart of Melbourne, Australia. Born from a passion for creating innovative digital solutions, we specialize in crafting high-quality software that makes a real difference in how businesses operate and grow.

Our approach combines technical excellence with human-centered design principles. We believe that the best software solutions aren't just technically sound – they're intuitive, accessible, and genuinely enjoyable to use. By maintaining a close collaboration with our clients throughout the development process, we ensure that every solution we deliver perfectly aligns with their needs and exceeds their expectations.

Our Vision

At Throwing Eights, we envision a future where technology seamlessly enhances human potential rather than complicating it. Our mission is to transform complex business challenges into elegant, user-friendly solutions that drive real value for our clients.

We're guided by three core principles:

  • Simplicity in Complexity: Making sophisticated technology accessible and intuitive
  • Human-First Design: Creating solutions that prioritize user experience and real-world usability
  • Sustainable Innovation: Building technology that not only solves today's problems but adapts to tomorrow's challenges

The Founders

Manu

A veteran software architect with over two decades of experience in building enterprise-scale applications, Manu brings technical excellence and strategic vision to Throwing Eights. His expertise in system design and passion for clean, efficient code forms the backbone of our technical approach.

With a background spanning multiple industries and technologies, Manu ensures that our solutions are not just cutting-edge, but also robust and scalable. His philosophy of "simplicity in design, excellence in execution" drives our development methodology.

Jamaica

Jamaica combines deep technical knowledge with a keen understanding of user experience design. Her approach to software development focuses on creating solutions that are both powerful and accessible, ensuring that complex functionality never comes at the cost of usability.

With expertise in front-end technologies and user interface design, Jamaica leads our efforts in creating software that doesn't just work well – it feels natural and intuitive to use. Her commitment to human-centered design principles ensures that our solutions truly serve the people who use them.

The Future

Looking ahead, Throwing Eights is positioned to be at the forefront of Melbourne's digital transformation landscape. We're investing in emerging technologies while maintaining our commitment to creating practical, user-focused solutions.

Our roadmap includes:

  • Expanding our expertise in AI and machine learning applications
  • Developing innovative solutions for sustainable technology practices
  • Growing our team of passionate technologists and problem-solvers
  • Strengthening our partnerships within Melbourne's tech community

We believe that the future of technology lies in its ability to simplify and enhance human experiences. As we grow, we remain committed to our core mission: delivering exceptional software solutions that make a real difference in how businesses and people work.
